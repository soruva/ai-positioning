About AI Positioning

AI Positioning is a decision-awareness tool designed to help individuals understand where they stand in the rapidly evolving AI economy.

Rather than simply testing knowledge, it evaluates how exposed a role is to automation and how well it aligns with future high-leverage opportunities.

The system analyzes factors such as adaptability, decision complexity, creativity, and human-centric skills to generate a positioning score.

Our mission is not to predict job loss — but to help people see their trajectory early, and take action before the shift becomes irreversible.

In a world where AI is reshaping every industry, clarity is leverage.

AI Positioning exists to give you that clarity.

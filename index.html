FAQ

Is this a real prediction?

This is a lightweight directional test. It is meant to help users think about AI exposure, not provide legal, financial, or career guarantees.

What makes a job more AI-safe?

Human trust, creativity, empathy, negotiation, leadership, and operating in unpredictable environments usually make work harder to automate fully.

Can I use this for teams or hiring discussions?

Yes. Many people use simple AI-risk tools as conversation starters for upskilling, workforce planning, and career transition thinking.

About

AI Job Test is a simple online tool that helps people estimate how vulnerable their job may be to automation and artificial intelligence.

It uses a short set of questions to estimate how much a role depends on human strengths such as empathy, creativity, judgment, and trust.

FAQ

Is this a real prediction?
This test gives a directional estimate about AI exposure. It is meant for discussion and exploration rather than exact prediction.
What makes a job more AI-safe?
Jobs requiring empathy, leadership, creativity, negotiation and strategic thinking tend to be harder for AI to automate fully.
Can companies use this tool?
Yes. Many teams use AI-risk quizzes to start conversations about reskilling and future workforce planning.
